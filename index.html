About Sunnybrook


Sunnybrook is a family-run landscaping business dedicated to quality craftsmanship, natural aesthetics, and reliable service. We’ve been helping homeowners and businesses across the region maintain beautiful outdoor spaces for over a decade.


Our approach combines horticultural knowledge with a passion for design — whether it’s a full garden makeover or routine maintenance.


Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to deliver green-focused landscaping solutions that make outdoor spaces both beautiful and sustainable. We believe that nature-conscious design should be available to everyone — homeowners, small businesses, and communities alike. Through thoughtful planning, careful craftsmanship, and a respect for the natural environment, we aim to create spaces that encourage connection, relaxation, and long-term ecological health.


VISION: Our vision is to become the region’s most trusted name in sustainable landscape transformation — known not only for our creativity and execution, but for our ethics, stewardship, and customer-first philosophy. We strive to shape environments that future generations can enjoy — greener neighborhoods, healthier ecosystems, and outdoor areas that feel inviting, personal, and alive.
